Airbnb, Inc. ( AIR-BEE-en-BEE, an abbreviation of its original name, "Air Bed and Breakfast") is an American company operating  an online marketplace for short-and-long-term homestays, experiences and services in various countries and regions. It acts as a broker and charges a commission from each booking. Airbnb was founded in 2008 by Brian Chesky, Nathan Blecharczyk, and Joe Gebbia. It is the best-known company for short-term housing rentals.
