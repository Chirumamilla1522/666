Equinix Inc. is an American multinational company headquartered in Redwood City, California. It specializes in internet connectivity and colocation centres, also referred to as carrier hotels. The company converted to a real estate investment trust (REIT) in January 2015.
A leader in global data centers, In 2025, its operations include 260 data centers in 33 countries on five continents. Equinix is listed on the Nasdaq stock exchange with the ticker symbol EQIX. It employs about 13,000 people worldwide as of December 2023.
